What can be shown in this type of brand kit?

Logos, typography, color rules, mockups, social assets, banners, business cards, and short brand guidelines.

Is it only for graphic design portfolios?

No. It can also support agencies, startups, product launches, and client presentation decks.

Can it be adapted for a website case study?

Yes. The same concept can be turned into a portfolio case study page with deeper project storytelling.

Why does it feel more client-ready?

Because the system includes clear grouping, presentation rhythm, and more usable brand context around the graphics.
